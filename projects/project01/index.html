Profile —

SHUSHU/TONG is a Shanghai based brand focusing on feminine style with a twist. Established by Liushu Lei and Yutong Jiang in 2015, SHUSHU/TONG features modern women who dares to express themselves by wearing unabashedly feminine details—ruffles, bowknots; but deflated of primness with relaxed and cool attitude.
